# Customer Revenue Intelligence & Churn Risk Analytics

## Overview

This project delivers an end-to-end analytics solution designed to evaluate revenue concentration, customer value distribution, and churn risk using transactional data.

It transforms raw purchase-level data into structured customer insights and presents them through an interactive business intelligence dashboard. The focus is on identifying high-value customers, understanding behavioral patterns, and quantifying revenue exposure from potential churn.

---

## Scope of Work

The project simulates a real-world analytics workflow, covering the full lifecycle from raw data processing to executive-level reporting:

- Cleaning and structuring transactional datasets  
- Engineering customer-level behavioral features  
- Performing RFM-based segmentation to classify customer value  
- Identifying churn based on inactivity patterns  
- Aggregating segment-level performance metrics  
- Building an interactive dashboard for stakeholder consumption  

---

## Analytical Framework

The analysis is built around three core pillars:

### 1. Customer Value Segmentation

Customers are segmented using the RFM model:

- **Recency** – How recently a customer made a purchase  
- **Frequency** – How often the customer transacts  
- **Monetary** – Total value contributed by the customer  

This enables classification into segments such as high-value, loyal, and at-risk customers.

---

### 2. Revenue Intelligence

The project evaluates how revenue is distributed across:

- Individual customers  
- Customer segments  
- Geographic regions  

This helps identify revenue concentration and dependency on specific customer groups.

---

### 3. Churn Risk Analysis

Churn is inferred using inactivity thresholds and analyzed across segments to determine:

- Which customer groups are most likely to churn  
- How churn risk varies by customer value  
- The potential revenue impact of customer loss  

---

## Dashboard Capabilities

The Power BI dashboard is structured to support business decision-making:

- **Executive Summary**  
  High-level KPIs including total revenue, customer base, order volume, and churn rate  

- **Revenue Structure Analysis**  
  Breakdown of revenue across segments and regions  

- **Customer Behavior Insights**  
  Visualization of RFM patterns and customer activity  

- **Churn & Risk Monitoring**  
  Identification of high-risk segments and associated revenue exposure  

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue is highly concentrated among a small subset of customers  
- High-value segments demonstrate significantly lower churn rates  
- Mid-tier segments contribute meaningful revenue but exhibit higher churn risk  
- A disproportionate share of revenue risk is linked to non-premium customer groups  

---

## Technology Stack

- Python – Data processing and feature engineering  
- Pandas / NumPy – Data manipulation and analysis  
- Jupyter Notebook – Analytical workflow  
- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboarding  

---

## Deliverables

- Processed and analysis-ready datasets  
- Customer-level segmentation and churn indicators  
- Segment-level performance summaries  
- Interactive Power BI dashboard for business insights
